Once upon a time, I was a computational seismologist in the Global Seismology Research Group at the Berkeley Seismological Laboratory. Here is a quick overview of my research interests and relevant conference proceedings and papers from that time.

Interests

  • Seismic waveform inversion (numerical wavefield simulation, inverse problems, etc.)
  • Parallel languages and runtimes (esp. partitioned global address space)
  • Application performance analysis (tools, models, etc.)
